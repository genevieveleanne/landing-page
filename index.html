Who Rescues Who?

Paws Up is a no-kill animal shelter in Northern Nevada that's dedicated to personalizing the adoption process. We find the best furever family for each dog and cat.

How Our Adoption Process Works

Step 1: Browse available dogs and cats.

Step 2: Fill out an application. An adoption counselor will contact you in 48 hours.

Step 3: If our adoption counselors think you are a fit for the animal, we will contact you to set up a meet-and-greet.

Step 4: If our adoption counselors do not think you are a fit, we will contact you to discuss the animal we believe will thrive in your home.
